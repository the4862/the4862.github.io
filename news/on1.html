GeForce Now Queues Are Fast Again

3/7/24






We are all used to the 300 person queues that took most of class to get into. After GeForce Now blew up it became pointless to use. You would wait in a queue for 45 minutes and play for 10 minutes. Nearly everyone has forgotten about or stopped using it now though. I was able to get into 3 different games in less than 15 minutes and the largest queue I got was about 30 people.

The queues may be back up for a while due to the new Fortnite update, but they still won't reach the old amounts like 300.
